Pure Veg, Pure Heart

What we do for you

At Sai-Vum, we believe in doing food differently, and doing it right. We're a 100% pure vegetarian Indian restaurant where simplicity meets authenticity, and flavor never compromises values.

  • Vegetarian for All : Our fully vegetarian kitchen respects religious and dietary restrictions. No cross-contamination with meat or eggs, ever, because they're not even in the building. We have verifications from IFANCA, AVA, and USDA for our food being halal, vegetarian, and organic respectively.
  • Wholesome Ingredients : We cook using real, organic, natural ingredients, just honest food made with love.
  • Affordable Goodness : Good food doesn't have to ed be expensive. While others charge more for processed food, we keep it simple and price right.
  • Clean & Quick : Our kitchen is sparkling clean and runs like clockwork. We prep in advance so you don't have to wait.

What we do for the world

We believe food should nourish not just the body, but the world around us. That's why every meal at Sai-Vum supports something bigger: We donate 30% of our profits to local charities because what goes around, comes around.

  • Farm to Table : We source directly from small, local farmers to support their livelihoods and bring you fresher food.
  • Eco-Friendly Always :
    • Our farm-to-table practices reduce fuel emissions from long-haul transport.
    • We use no disposable plates or cutlery for dine-in customers.
    • Takeout? We've got you covered with biodegradable containers made from bagasse (sugarcane pulp).
    • Any unsold food is donated to local food banks feeding those who need it most.
